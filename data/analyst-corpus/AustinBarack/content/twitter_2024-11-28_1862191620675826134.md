TVL isn’t important if you don’t account for growth. High TVL and decelerating or no growth is bearish. Sounds obvious but often ignored. 

Also TVL is typically a much slower moving indicator. Volumes and revenue lead.

Following this avoids 90% of fundamental investing traps.
