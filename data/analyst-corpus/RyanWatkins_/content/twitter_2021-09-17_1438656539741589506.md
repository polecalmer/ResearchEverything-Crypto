### Tweet 3/17
**@RyanWatkins_** | 2021-09-17 | [link](https://x.com/RyanWatkins_/status/1438656548138586115)

Why do stablecoins continue to find product market fit?

- They’re easy to accept as payments given that all you need is an address on a public blockchain

- They run on global public infrastructure that operates 24/7/365 which makes them available, cheap, and reliable to use

The offshore case.

Stablecoins could service offshore dollar demand by providing individuals and institutions around the world with easy access to dollars due to stablecoins being natively digital, globally accessible, and more seizure resistant.

The domestic case stablecoins.

Over time financial institutions shift more transactions to blockchains due to:

1. Properties explained above (24/7, programmable, cheap, reliable, etc).

2. Fundamentally leaner cost structure from shifting capex (infrastructure) to blockchains.
