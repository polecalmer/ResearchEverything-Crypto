### Tweet 3/8

The stablecoin stack is primarily composed of stablecoin issuers (i.e., Tether) and stablecoin distributors (i.e., CeFi/DeFi apps, payment apps, wallets)

While the distributors disproportionately create value on behalf of the issuers, they currently capture none of it https://t.co/mUcsADIF7r
