### Tweet 3/5
**@shaundadevens** | 2023-04-19 | [link](https://x.com/shaundadevens/status/1648657742851129345)

Holding an Asset is equivalent to purchasing that asset at the current price. Don't fall victim to the classic sunk cost fallacy - as a Holder always ask yourself : If I wasn't holding this coin currently would I still buy it?

If the answer is no - reallocate.
