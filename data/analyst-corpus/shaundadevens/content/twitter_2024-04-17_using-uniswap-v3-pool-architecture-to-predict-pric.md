### Tweet 2/12
**@shaundadevens** | 2024-04-17 | [link](https://x.com/shaundadevens/status/1780666979289849918)

How does this lead to reflexivity in price?

Large price movements lead to imbalances in pools, making LPs out of range with 100% of one asset,

Out of range LPs will ONLY buy or sell , to get back into range. 

I.e 100% UNI LP will ONLY sell to get back in range. https://t.co/JVuJnNSjyQ

### Tweet 3/12
**@shaundadevens** | 2024-04-17 | [link](https://x.com/shaundadevens/status/1780666976525762639)

Uniswap Lps allow for concentrated liquidity to be set within ranges. 

Assume $UNI is at $15. A user can provide $1000 in UNI/USD LP with a 50/50 Ratio with a range between $10 and $20. 

At $10 the LP is completely in UNI, At $20 the LP is completely in USDC.

### Tweet 4/12
**@shaundadevens** | 2024-04-17 | [link](https://x.com/shaundadevens/status/1780666982385209655)

Price dumps create large sell walls as seen here, as out of range LPs are now purely $UNI and need to Sell UNI to rebalance. 

These out of range LPs are posting $20m worth of sell orders before $13.5 / UNI , while there is only $2m of TOTAL buy orders for $UNI. https://t.co/xpbfeRrP7Z

### Tweet 5/12
**@shaundadevens** | 2024-04-17 | [link](https://x.com/shaundadevens/status/1780666987531608263)

The opposite effect is true for price pumps. To illustrate

at $10 - LP is completely in UNI, will sell UNI until $20.
at $20 - LP is completely in USD, will buy UNI until $10

Further LP providers might manually rebalance by selling, e.g selling 50% of UNI to create a new LP. https://t.co/ZKMIjg3Zn9

### Tweet 6/12
**@shaundadevens** | 2024-04-17 | [link](https://x.com/shaundadevens/status/1780666984784363567)

While passive orders don't move the price, this $20m sell pressure makes it harder for price to move upwards.

In contrast, there are extremely little buy orders meaning that sell orders require little volume to lower price.

In effect sells have 10x price impact as buys

### Tweet 7/12
**@shaundadevens** | 2024-04-17 | [link](https://x.com/shaundadevens/status/1780666989905605010)

This leads to a reflexive cycle where coins that already pumped will continue to pump as UNI LPs are imbalanced and post passive buy orders - While coins that dumped rebalance by posting sell orders. 

pair this with sentiment and you understand why coins can pump/dump so hard.
