2/ AMM liquidity pools work on a constant product rule:
x · y = k (where x = USDC in the pool, y = LIGHT in the pool, k = invariant product).

When someone buys LIGHT with USDC:

USDC (x) is added to the pool
LIGHT (y) is removed from the pool
The new price is simply p = x / y
